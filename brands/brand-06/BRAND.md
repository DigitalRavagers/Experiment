## Voice & Tone

- **Personality**: Mystical, earthy, curated — high-end spiritual aesthetic
- **Tone**: Warm and knowing — "these stones carry energy you can feel"
- **Vocabulary**: Use: energetic, grounding, intention, healing, sacred / Avoid: pseudoscience claims, generic wellness buzzwords

## Audience

- **ICP**: Spiritually-oriented consumers seeking energetic/healing products; particularly strong market in Brazil (gemstones) and India (spiritual products)
- **Key Pain Points**:
  1. Saturated market of generic spiritual products with no authentic storytelling
  2. Consumers want provenance and meaning behind their purchases
  3. International buyers want curated, premium-quality gemstone sourcing

## Positioning

- **Category**: Curated spiritual and energetic product brand
- **Differentiator**: Unique brand positioning in spiritual/energetic product space + storytelling-led brand identity
- **Market Focus**: Brazil for gemstones, India for spiritual products, global English-speaking spiritual community
- **Strategy**: Lead magnet + community building → product sales
