## Voice & Tone

- **Personality**: Bold, systems-oriented, cutting-edge, no-fluff
- **Tone**: Confident and direct — "we handle the heavy lifting so you scale effortlessly"
- **Vocabulary**: Use: automation, AI agents, scale, systems, done-for-you / Avoid: corporate jargon, vague buzzwords
- **Analogies**: Digital Ravagers is the SEAL team for your business operations

## Audience

- **ICP**: Solopreneurs, small agency owners, entrepreneurs wanting to automate marketing, content, and operations without hiring a full team
- **Demographics**: Ages 25–45, online business owners, digital-first
- **Key Pain Points**:
  1. Wearing too many hats — no time to execute consistently
  2. Paying for multiple tools/subscriptions without a unified system
  3. Missing technical knowledge to implement AI automation themselves

## Positioning

- **Category**: AI-powered done-for-you brand + automation agency
- **Differentiator**: 50/50 revenue-share with clients + full AI agent fleet — skin in the game, not a retainer
- **Tagline**: "We don't just build systems. We run them."
- **Elevator Pitch**: Digital Ravagers builds and deploys AI automation ecosystems for brands that want to scale without scaling headcount. We take a revenue share, not a retainer — our growth is tied to yours.

## Three Core Offerings

1. **Brand Creation** — Brand strategy, identity, positioning, done-for-you
2. **AI Automation** — Custom n8n/agent workflows for marketing, CRM, social, content
3. **Done-For-You Business Solutions** — Full-stack agency ops for client brands

## Revenue Model

- 50/50 revenue share on client brands
- Team: 3–5% of revenue per brand they work on
- Own digital products (courses, tools) under Digital Ravagers ecosystem
